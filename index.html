Biography

Fatemeh Akbar received her Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA, in 2018. Afterwards, she joined the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) as a postdoctoral research scholar. She is currently an assistant professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Sharif University of Technology.

Ms. Akbar received the Engineering Graduate Symposium Technical Award from the University of Michigan's College of Engineering in 2014, 2016, and 2017, and was selected as the excellent graduate student instructor by the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Ms. Akbar received the Engineering Graduate Symposium Technical Award from the University of Michigan's College of Engineering in 2014, 2016, and 2017. She was also named a Rising Star by MIT's EECS in 2018 and was selected as an excellent graduate student instructor by the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Michigan in Winter 2018

Her research interests include RF/mmWave/THz IC Design, Wireline Communications, Ultra-low-power IC Design, Applied Electromagnetics, and Bioelectronics.
