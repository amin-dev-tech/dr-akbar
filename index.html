About Me

Fatemeh Akbar received her Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA, in 2018. Afterwards, she joined the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) as a postdoctoral research scholar, and she is currently serving as an assistant professor at the Electrical Engineering department of the Sharif University of Technology.

Her research interests include RF/mmWave/THz IC Design, Wireline Communications, Ultra-low-power IC Design, Applied Electromagnetics, and Bioelectronics.
